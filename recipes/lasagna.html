Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Beef and Sausage 1lb each
  • One Egg
  • Italian seasoning, 1/8 cup.

Why make this?

Tender sheets of pasta, a creamy ricotta cheese filling and a rich meaty

tomato sauce are all topped with more cheese and baked until browned and bubbly.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. Boil noodles according to package directions.
  2. Brown beef and sausage in a large skillet, drain fat.
  3. Stir in pasta sauce and italian seasoning.
  4. Simmer uncovered on medium heat for 5 minutes or until thickened.
  5. In a seperate bowl combine the cheeses and egg with a pinch of seasoning until creamy.
  6. Spread meat sauce in 9x13 pan, lay noodles on top, spread cheese. Repeat until no ingredients.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  8. Remove from foil and sprinkle extra cheese on and bake for another 15 minutes.
  9. Rest for 15 minutes, then enjoy your lasagna!

Air-Fryer Waffle Egg Hole

Air-fryer Tilapia

Why make this?

You'll love the sweet and savory flavors in this easy, all-in-one breakfast. This fun twist

on classic egg-in-a-hole is air fried for easy cooking and clean-up.

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen waffle
  • 1 large egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons shredded cheese of your choice
  • Maple syrup to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 350 degrees F
  2. Cut a hole in the center of the waffle using the rim of a glass.
  3. Carefully place the waffle on parchment paper along with the cut out hole.
  4. Crack egg directly on to the center hole, season as needed.
  5. Close lid and air fry until egg is set, about 5 to 6 minutes.
  6. Open fryer to sprinkle cheese and cook until melted.
  7. Transfer waffle to plate and enjoy!
